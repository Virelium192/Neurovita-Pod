Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the NeoroVita Pod.

How much space is required for the NeoroVita Pod?

The NeoroVita Pod has a compact footprint of approximately 4.5' x 4.5' with a height of 5.5'. We recommend allowing at least 1-2 feet of additional space around the pod for easy access. The pod can be placed against a wall to save space if needed.

How long does a typical wellness session last?

A standard session ranges from 10-25 minutes, depending on the selected program and your specific wellness needs. Quick 5-minute stress relief sessions are also available for busy environments, while comprehensive wellness assessments may take up to 30 minutes.

Can the pod be used by multiple people?

Yes, the NeoroVita Pod is designed to support multiple user profiles, making it ideal for families, offices, and wellness centers. Each user can create their own profile, which saves their preferences, wellness history, and personalized programs. The Professional and Enterprise models allow for unlimited user profiles.

What maintenance is required for the pod?

The NeoroVita Pod is designed for minimal maintenance. Regular upkeep includes wiping down surfaces with the provided cleaning kit, replacing the aromatherapy cartridges every 2-3 months (depending on usage), and keeping the software updated through the automatic update system. We recommend a professional check-up once a year, which is included in our maintenance plans.

Is any special electrical setup required?

The NeoroVita Pod operates on standard 110-240V electricity and can be plugged into a regular wall outlet. It consumes approximately the same amount of power as a desktop computer. For the Enterprise model with multiple pods, we provide consultation on optimal electrical setup for your specific environment.
